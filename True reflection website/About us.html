About our company

Vision

To be one of the leading service provider in Limpopo, Gauteng, Northwest province and Mpumalanga Province.

Mission

To maintain mutual relationship and quality servicesto our clientele.

Objectives

  • To contribute to the South African economy
  • To providequality services
  • To perform thequality work at an agreed time

Core values

The values of our business relies on:

  • Professionalism
  • Confidentiality
  • Honesty
  • Respect and Dignity

Market Stratergy

We deliver quality products at an affordable price.

NETWORKING /LINKAGES

Truereflection (Pty) Ltd will establish links with the public or government institutions and private sector around Polokwane Local Municipality and Waterberg District.

The business will in future also focus on creating linkages within the Limpopo, North West, Mpumalanga, Gauteng and South Africa at large. This will also expand to SADEC countries should the need arise.

FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT

The business provides a service only if a valid order has beenreceived. No cash will be accepted, but only bank deposits slip, a banktransfer and or payment stub will be accepted after payment.

SUSTAINABILITY

The business will sustain itself by giving quality service, reasonable pricing and strive to create and maintain a long-term relationship with clients. Word of mouth approach will be mostly used as a main form of advertisement. The Managing member will present the concept to various institutions.
